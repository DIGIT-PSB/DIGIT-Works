Past DIGIT Works Presentations

DIGIT Works is held regularly at the end of Fall and Spring semesters, and this series began in Fall 2020. This page preserves an archive of all DIGIT Works schedules back to that point, and the schedule pages found here include links provided to projects. Caution: some project sites and code repositories may not have been maintained, but we preserve what record we have of their presentations. Please contact Dr. Beshero-Bondar with questions or updates about the projects represented here.

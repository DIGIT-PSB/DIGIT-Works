What is DIGIT Works?

DIGIT students from Penn State Erie, The Behrend College present their semester and senior design projects. The projects represent work in progress, nearing completion from senior projects, Digit and Game courses taught by Profs. Tommy Hartung, Elisa Beshero-Bondar, Lauren Liebe, Joel Hunt, Lisa Jo Elliott, and Pulin Agrawal.

When/Where is DIGIT Works?

When: May 1, 2026: 12:00pm - 6:30pm. Where: The event is hybrid! You may join us either

  • In Person: VARIA Lab (Downstairs Lilley Library)
  • Virtual (Zoom): Zoom registration.

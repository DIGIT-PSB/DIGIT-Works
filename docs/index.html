What is DIGIT Works?

DIGIT students from Penn State Erie, The Behrend College present their semester and senior design projects. The projects represent work in progress, nearing completion from Senior Capstone projects (DIGIT 494), Large-Scale Text Analysis (Digit 210), Advanced Digital Creations (Digit 409), Fundamentals of Digital Audio (INART 258A), Data Visualization (Digit 410), Game Development Projects (Game 480), Technical Game Development (Game 250), taught by Profs. Tommy Hartung, Lisa Jo Elliott, Lauren Liebe, Pulin Agrawal, Elisa Beshero-Bondar, and Joel Hunt.
